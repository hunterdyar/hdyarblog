---
title: I published a Dice Rolling app ...9 Years Ago
date: 2025-10-09
---

9 years ago, I published a [dice rolling app](https://apps.repebble.com/en_US/application/5791726850c752e32e0000e1?query=dice%2520roller&section=watchapps) for pebbles on the pebble app store. Today, that app... still exists!

Today, the [revival](https://repebble.com/) of Pebble continues, and Eric Migicovsky [announced](https://ericmigi.com/blog/re-introducing-the-pebble-appstore) that the new appstore is the same as the old appstore. 
Old pebble apps will continue to be supported on the [new devices](https://store.repebble.com/).

As a massive fan of Pebble, it's great to see my old app still around. 

It wasn't the only Dice rolling app, and it probably wasn't the best - but it wasn't the worst either. I am not particularly proud of design of this old weekend project, but I am proud that I published something, and that it's somehow still kicking. Very cool too see.
